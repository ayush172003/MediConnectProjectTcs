Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Search for a doctor on the home page, select a specialist, login to your patient account, and pick an available slot.

Can I view my old prescriptions?

Yes, all prescriptions are automatically saved in your 'Medical History' tab, grouped by the doctor who issued them.
